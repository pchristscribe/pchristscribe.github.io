The Trevor Project

As a teenager who really struggled with the coming out process, I have long had a special place in my heart for The Trevor Project. They advocate for LGBT youth and offer crisis support 24/7/365. They have fully embraced technology too, developing an AI tool in collaboration with Google,org to train counselors to prepare them for supporting youth in crisis. In it’s first year, my birthday fundraiser raised over $200, but you don’t have to wait til then to give back to a great cause. Donate Today!
